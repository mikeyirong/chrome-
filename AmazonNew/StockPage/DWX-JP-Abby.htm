Follow the steps below to fix your stranded inventory in bulk by uploading an inventory file

Step 1. Request and download your Bulk Fix Stranded Inventory report on this page.
Step 2. Open the report in a spreadsheet program and update the price and condition of your products. Learn more
Step 3. Upload your updated inventory file in the Add Products via Upload page. If you use Amazon Marketplace Web Services, you also have the option of uploading the information using an Inventory Loader Template.

Follow the steps below to configure your restock settings in bulk by updating and emailing an inventory file

Step 1. Request and download your Restock Inventory settings file on this page.
Step 2. Open the file in a spreadsheet program and update the restock settings for each product.
Step 3. Email your updated inventory file to inventoryplanningbeta@amazon.com. It may take up to two business days for your Restock Inventory page to reflect these changes.
